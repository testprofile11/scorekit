J1 Hoops Scoreboard

Open the scoreboard, then install it to your iPhone Home Screen from Safari.

Open Scoreboard
  1. Upload this folder to an HTTPS web host.
  2. Open this installer page in Safari on iPhone.
  3. Tap Open Scoreboard.
  4. Tap Safari Share.
  5. Tap Add to Home Screen.
iPhone does not allow normal downloaded app installer files unless the app is distributed through TestFlight, the App Store, or an Apple-signed enterprise system.
